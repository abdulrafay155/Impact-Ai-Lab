Teaching

Courses, teaching philosophy, and educational resources

Teaching Philosophy

Dr. Divya Sharma is committed to providing high-quality education that combines theoretical foundations with practical applications. Her teaching approach emphasizes critical thinking, problem-solving, and hands-on experience with real-world data and applications.

She believes in creating an inclusive learning environment where students from diverse backgrounds can thrive and develop the skills needed for success in academia and industry.

Current Courses

Machine Learning for Healthcare Fall 2023

This course explores the application of machine learning techniques to healthcare data, with a focus on predictive modeling, patient risk stratification, and clinical decision support systems.

Topics covered: Supervised and unsupervised learning, deep learning for medical imaging, natural language processing for clinical text, ethical considerations in healthcare AI.

Advanced Statistical Methods Winter 2023

This course covers advanced statistical methods with applications in data science, focusing on both theoretical foundations and practical implementation.

Topics covered: Bayesian statistics, time series analysis, survival analysis, causal inference, and statistical learning theory.
